Our Production
Our production of furniture is second to none! We craft pieces from the finest materials, making sure that each item is built to last.

We take great pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail. Our furniture is designed to bring beauty and comfort to any home. Every piece is finished with an exquisite finish that makes it stand out from the crowd.


Our furniture and decoration pieces are perfect for any room in the house, from the living room to the bedroom to the dining room.

Our team of skilled craftsmen put a lot of care into each piece of furniture to ensure that each one is durable and stylish. We offer a wide range of furniture styles, from traditional to modern, so you're sure to find something that fits your taste.


Whether you're looking for a new sofa, a dining table, or a custom piece, our furniture manufacturing business is here to help.

At FHome, we use up-to-date technologies and approaches to ensure we provide the highest quality furniture. We use a combination of traditional woodworking techniques and modern processes to create beautiful pieces of furniture that are both durable and stylish.


Our furniture and decoration items are crafted using a combination of automated machinery and skilled craftsmanship.

We use state-of-the-art CNC machines to cut, shape, and join the wood pieces and then hand-finish the pieces. We also use advanced computer-aided design (CAD) software to create 3D models of our furniture designs.


This allows us too quickly and accurately create furniture pieces. We take great pride in our furniture and use only the finest materials. We also use eco-friendly glues and finishes to ensure our furniture is safe.

History of Our Furniture Company
Our business has come a long way and continues to grow.

1995 — Our company was founded by John Smith in a small workshop.

1998 — We moved to a larger facility to accommodate our growing team and production capabilities.

2001 — Our company expanded its product line to include custom-made furniture pieces for corporate clients.

2005 — We opened our first retail showroom in London to offer our furniture directly to consumers.

2010 — Our company implemented environmentally friendly practices, including the use of sustainable materials and energy-efficient machinery.

2023 — We continue to provide high-quality furniture to our clients and remain committed to sustainable and ethical manufacturing practices.
